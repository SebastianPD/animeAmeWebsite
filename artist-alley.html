Apply as an Artist

Interested in selling your work at Anime Ame's Artist Alley? We'd love to have you! Here's what you need to know:

Table Information

  • Tables are available in 8x10 sizes
  • Each table includes two chairs and a standard tablecloth
  • Electrical access is available
  • WiFi is provided for all Artist Alley participants

How to Apply

Applications for Artist Alley will open soon. Watch our social media channels for announcements! When applications open, you'll need to provide:

  • Portfolio or samples of your work
  • Description of what you plan to sell
  • Any special setup requirements

Rules & Guidelines

  • Fan art of copyrighted characters is permitted for personal artistic expression
  • No AI-generated artwork
  • Artists must be present at their table during operating hours
  • Keep your area clean and respect neighboring tables
  • Pricing must be clearly displayed on all items
