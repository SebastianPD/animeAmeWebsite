Get Involved

Join Anime Ame and make the convention shine!

Cosplay Guest

Are you a cosplayer with multiple wins, years of experience, and a dedicated following? We'd love to feature you as a Cosplay Guest at Anime Ame! Our cosplay guests receive complimentary weekend passes, premium seating at events, a private room, and a featured spot in our promotional materials. You will also have the option to host a panel or even be a cosplay judge!

What we're looking for:

  • Active social media presence in the cosplay community i.e still making cosplays
  • Experience in cosplay contests and various crafting mediums and methods
  • Availability for a meet-and-greet session during the convention

Interested? Send your portfolio and social media links to guests@animeame.com

Content Creator

Whether you're on YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, or Twitch, if you are a social media creator covering anime and Japanese pop culture, we want you at Anime Ame! Content creators will receive press-level access to enter panels and events early, a private room, and have the option to host a panel.

Requirements:

  • Active social media presence on primary social media platform. Instagram and TikTok post once a week. Youtube and Twitch post/stream once a month.
  • Meet our minimum engagement to follower threshold. Varies per platform. Thresholds not disclosed to prevent botting.
  • Original content that focuses on anime, manga, cosplay, anime film. Meme accounts and reposters need not apply.
  • Be able to post promotional content before, during, and/or after the con. You will be assigned a time block to post your content.

Benefits include:

Apply with your channel links and content samples at creators@animeame.com

Local Press

Members of the media and press are welcome to cover Anime Ame. We offer press passes with access to all public convention areas, press-exclusive interview opportunities, and a private room for writing and editing.

To apply for a press pass, please provide:

  • Name of your outlet or publication
  • Links to previous convention coverage, if any.
  • Expected coverage plan (articles, video, photography, etc.)
  • Number of press passes requested (maximum 2 per outlet)

Submit your press application to press@animeame.com

Volunteer

Anime Ame needs amazing volunteers like you! Volunteering is a fantastic way to be part of the convention, gain behind-the-scenes experience, and be at the forefront of your community. Plus, you'll earn some great perks!

Volunteer perks:

  • Free weekend pass for shifts of 6+ hours across the convention
  • Free Anime Ame sweatshirt or hoodie
  • Volunteer-only lounge with snacks and refreshments

Available volunteer roles:

  • Registration desk and badge check
  • Panel room management
  • Artist Alley assistance
  • Gaming zone attendant
  • Accessibility and guest services
  • General event support

Sign up to volunteer at volunteer@animeame.com — we can't wait to have you on the team!
