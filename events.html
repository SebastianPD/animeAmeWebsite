Events & Activities

Two days packed with gaming, performances, panels, and more

What to Do at Anime Ame

🎮 Video Game Zone

Step into our gaming area featuring retro classics and the latest releases. A maze of machines and stations. Be part of major fighting game tournaments!

🎲 Tabletop Section

Roll dice, draw cards, and strategize with fellow fans in our dedicated Tabletop Section. We have a huge library of board games and card games available to play, plus open tables for your own games. 

📚 Manga Library

Need a break from the excitement? Relax in our cozy Manga Library featuring many volumes across every genre. Grab a beanbag, pick a series, and relax. 

🎤 Idol Show — May 9

Don't miss the highlight of Saturday evening! Our Idol Show features talented performers bringing electrifying dance routines and vocal performances to the main stage. Cheer on your favorites and experience the energy of a live idol performance. Show starts at 6:00 PM on May 9th.

👘 Cosplay Contest — May 10

Sunday's main event! The Anime Ame Cosplay Contest showcases the most incredible costumes and performances from our talented community. Multiple categories, expert judges, and amazing prizes await. Contest begins at 2:00 PM on May 10th. See the Cosplay page for rules and registration.

📋 Panels & Workshops

From anime discussion panels to cosplay crafting workshops, to Japanese culture presentations, our panel rooms are packed with content all weekend. Check the schedule below for the full lineup.
