Health & Safety

Your well-being is our top priority

Health Policies

Anime Ame is committed to providing a safe and healthy environment for all attendees, staff, and volunteers. We monitor public health guidelines and adjust our policies accordingly.

  • Masks — Masks are welcomed and encouraged but not required. Please respect the personal space of others at con.
  • Hand Sanitizer — Hand sanitizing stations are placed throughout the venue for your convenience.
  • Feeling Unwell? — We kindly ask that you stay home. Standard refund and transfer policies apply.
  • First Aid — A first aid station staffed by trained medical personnel is available throughout the convention. Located near the main registration area.
  • Medication Storage — Refrigerated medication storage is available at the first aid station. Ask at registration for details.

Safety & Anti-Harassment Policy

Anime Ame has a zero-tolerance policy for harassment of any kind. Every attendee deserves to feel safe, respected, and welcome. This policy applies to all attendees, panelists, vendors, volunteers, and staff.

What Constitutes Harassment

  • Unwanted physical contact or intimidation
  • Verbal abuse, slurs, or discriminatory language
  • Stalking or following someone after being asked to stop
  • Photographing or recording someone without consent
  • Sexual harassment in any form
  • Deliberate disruption of panels, events, or other convention activities
  • Display of offensive or hateful imagery

Reporting Harassment

If you experience or witness harassment, please report it immediately:

  • Visit the Convention Operations desk near the main entrance
  • Speak to any staff member or volunteer wearing an "Anime Ame Staff" badge
  • Email safety@animeame.com

All reports are taken seriously and handled promptly. Consequences for harassment may include ejection from the convention without refund and being banned from future events.

Emergency Information

  • Convention Operations — Available at the main registration desk during all convention hours
  • First Aid Station — Located near main registration, staffed by trained personnel
  • Emergency Services — Call 911 for life-threatening emergencies
  • Nearest Hospital — Overlake Medical Center, 1035 116th Ave NE, Bellevue (approx. 5 minutes by car)

Code of Conduct Summary

By attending Anime Ame, you agree to:

  • Treat all attendees, staff, and volunteers with respect and kindness
  • Follow the directions of convention staff and security
  • Comply with all posted rules and guidelines
  • Report any safety concerns to staff immediately
  • Respect the venue, its property, and the property of others
  • Have fun and help create a positive experience for everyone!
