Getting There

The Meydenbauer Center is conveniently located in downtown Bellevue with multiple transportation options:

  • By Car — Multiple parking garages are available within walking distance. The convention has the Bravern Public Garage underneath which is $26 if you stay over 6 hours.
  • By Light Rail — The Bellevue Downtown station is right in front of the venue!
  • By Bus — Multiple King County Metro routes serve downtown Bellevue. Check the Metro website for routes and schedules.

Accessible Parking

ADA-compliant parking spaces are available in the the Bravern Public Garage with direct elevator access to the convention level. Additional accessible parking is available in nearby garages.
