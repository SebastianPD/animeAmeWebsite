Accessibility

An inclusive experience for all

Our Accessibility Mission

At Anime Ame, we believe that every fan deserves the full convention experience. We are committed to creating an inclusive environment where attendees of all abilities can navigate the convention, attend panels, participate in contests, and enjoy every moment without barriers.

Our Accommodations

  • Wheelchair & Mobility Access — All convention spaces are fully wheelchair accessible. Ramps, elevators, and wide pathways ensure smooth navigation throughout the Meydenbauer Center.
  • Priority Seating — Reserved accessible seating is available at all panels, the idol show, and the cosplay contest. No need to arrive early or worry about finding a spot.
  • Rest Areas — Dedicated quiet rest areas are available throughout the venue for attendees who need a break from the excitement. These spaces are climate-controlled and comfortable.
  • Chronic Condition Support — We provide refrigerated medication storage, a private first-aid room, and staff trained to assist attendees with chronic conditions.
  • Sensory-Friendly Spaces — Low-stimulation rooms are available for attendees who may become overwhelmed by noise, crowds, or bright lights.
  • ASL Interpretation — American Sign Language interpreters are available at major panels and events. 
  • Contest Accommodations — Cosplay contest participants with accessibility needs will receive full support on stage, including ramp access, extended time, and any other accommodations needed to perform at their best.
  • Service Animals — Service animals are welcome throughout the convention. Water stations for service animals are available at key locations.
  • Accessible Parking — ADA-compliant parking spaces are available at the Meydenbauer Center, with direct access to the convention entrance.

Need Accommodations?

If you have specific accessibility needs that aren't listed above, please don't hesitate to reach out. We are dedicated to working with you individually to ensure your convention experience is comfortable and enjoyable.

Contact our accessibility team at accessibility@animeame.com and we'll work with you to make your experience the best it can be.
