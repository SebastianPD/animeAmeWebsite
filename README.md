# animeAmeWebsite
Visit [animeame.org](animeame.org)

## Why I made this site
One of my hobbies is cosplay. I have made suits, entered competitions, and go to conventions regularly. Over the past few months many congoers, including myself, have been frustrated with the directions cons are heading. Increasing ticket prices, prioritizing selling merch and autographs of celebrities than entertainment like panels and cosplay contests and ignoring requests from people with disabilities to be more accommodating. I had a chat with a friend about it and it had us fantasizing about what we would want in our own con. I then did research into making a nonprofit and I took advantage of this assignment to make my own website for my fake con and do more research into what makes a good convention.
